‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does account deletion take?

Most deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email when your data has been completely deleted.

Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?

No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, we cannot restore your account or data.

Q: What happens to my exam results?

Your exam results will be deleted. However, if you're part of an educational institution, they may retain anonymized exam records separately for their own compliance purposes.

Q: I didn't receive the verification email. What should I do?

Check your spam/junk folder. If you still don't see it, contact us at privacy@ultimatedigitalsolutions.in with your registered email address.

Q: Will deletion affect other users in my organization?

No, only your personal account and data will be deleted. Other users' accounts and shared organizational data remain unaffected.

Q: Can I delete just some of my data instead of my entire account?

Yes! For partial data deletion requests, contact us at privacy@ultimatedigitalsolutions.in with specific details about what data you'd like removed.

Q: What if I'm part of an educational institution?

Contact your institution's administrator first. They may need to remove you from their system before you can delete your account. Some educational records may be retained by the institution.
