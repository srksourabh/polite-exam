Privacy Policy for Polite Coaching Centre

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Polite Coaching Centre ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you use our app:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email Address: For account creation and communication
  • Mobile Number: For account verification and contact purposes
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely for account access

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Exam Performance: Your test scores, answers, and completion times
  • Progress Data: Your learning progress and exam history
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Statistics: Features used and time spent on the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide exam services and track your progress
  • To generate performance reports and analytics
  • To improve our app and services
  • To communicate with you about updates and important information
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously. Your information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Secure Database: Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases (Airtable)
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@ultimatedigitalsolutions.in

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain certain information for:

  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Resolution of disputes
  • Enforcement of agreements
  • Aggregate analytics (anonymized)

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
