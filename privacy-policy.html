1. Introduction

Welcome to Polite Coaching Centre's Exam Management System ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide when using our App:

  • Student Information: Name, roll number, class/standard, contact details
  • Exam Data: Exam results, scores, answers, test submissions
  • Account Credentials: Login credentials for admin, student, and examiner roles
  • Contact Information: Email addresses, phone numbers (if provided)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Exam Management: To conduct online exams, manage question banks, and generate results
  • Student Progress Tracking: To track student performance and provide personalized feedback
  • Document Processing: To process scanned documents using OCR technology
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
  • Communication: To send exam notifications, results, and important updates
  • Security: To prevent fraud, maintain security, and protect user accounts
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and educational regulations

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access Control: Strict role-based access control (Admin, Student, Examiner)
  • Data Backup: Regular automated backups to prevent data loss
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, industry-standard cloud infrastructure
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law:

  • Active Students: Data retained for the duration of enrollment + 2 years
  • Exam Records: Retained for 5 years for academic record purposes

  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.
