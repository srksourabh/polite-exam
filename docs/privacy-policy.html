1. Introduction

Ultimate Digital Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Polite Coaching Centre mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Optional profile photo, date of birth, educational background
  • Communication Data: Messages sent to support team, feedback, and queries

2.2 Exam and Performance Data

  • Exam attempts, scores, and results
  • Subject-wise performance analytics
  • Time spent on exams and questions
  • Progress tracking and improvement metrics

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, app interactions
  • Log Data: IP address, crash reports, error logs for debugging

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, authenticate login
  • Service Delivery: Provide exam functionality, track progress, generate reports
  • Performance Analytics: Generate personalized insights and improvement recommendations
  • Communication: Send exam notifications, updates, and support responses
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, ensure app security
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features
  • Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to help operate our App:

  • Airtable: Secure cloud database for storing user data and exam records
  • Vercel: Hosting infrastructure for backend services
  • Email Services: For sending notifications and support communications

These providers have access only to data necessary for their functions and are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt algorithm
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on Airtable's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

6.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is immediately removed from active systems
  • Exam history and performance data are permanently deleted
  • Backup systems are purged within 90 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes

You can request account deletion anytime through the app or at delete-account.html

6.3 Legal Retention

For legal compliance (fraud prevention, regulatory requirements), we may retain certain anonymized data for up to 7 years as required by Indian law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@politecoaching.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on cloud servers that may be located outside India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws including GDPR for European users.

10. Third-Party Links

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for European users
  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
