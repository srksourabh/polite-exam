How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete Within the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Polite Coaching Centre app
  2. Go to your Profile or Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
  5. Your account will be deleted immediately

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to: support@politecoaching.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Email address registered with the app
    • Mobile number (if registered)
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, mobile number, profile photo
  • Exam Data: All exam attempts, scores, and performance analytics
  • Progress Reports: Subject-wise performance history and improvement tracking
  • Study Materials: Any saved notes, bookmarks, or downloaded content
  • Settings & Preferences: All app settings and customizations
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and session tokens
